This was a compensation and benefits survey that was sent to graduate students on behalf of GSA (specifically
the Advocacy Committee) during the 2024-2025 school year through Qualtrics. The responses to this survey
formed the basis for the higher pay resolution that was subsequently written, voted on, and passed during
spring semester of the 2024-2025 school year.
